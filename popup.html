HIIL: High Intensity Interval Learning

Like HIIT, but for learning.

It 10Xs your learning gains.

Total Time = How long you'll study/practice for
Focus Time = How long you'll focus before the rest interval
Rest Time = How long the rest interval will be

Dr. Andrew Huberman recommends 10s at random per every 2min (~1:10) of focused learning/practicing time.

Good luck!
